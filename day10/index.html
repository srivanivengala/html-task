Investment Process and Philosophy

Fincorp utilises a very disciplined process of investment, starting from effective Fund Management to strict Compliance, Back Office systems and efficient Client Servicing. Our aim has always been to provide the client with superior returns against low levels of volatility, taking into consideration the risks of the economy and market liquidity as seen in the MSX.

Our philosophy of investment is based on a bottom-up stock picking methodology. However, given the strong macro relationship with the corporate bottom line, we use a blended matrix of both macro top-down and individual bottom-up stock picking methodologies.

Our investment philosophy is therefore flexible to meet the changes and challenges of this market.

However, we never compromise on stock liquidity and fundamentals of the company before allocation. Trading is used only in a minor way to provide excess returns but never as a major strategy. Therefore, our clients make more than normal returns on stock allocation and lower churning (low brokerage).
